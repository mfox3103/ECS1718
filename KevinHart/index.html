Kevin Hart


Kevin Hart is an American comedian, actor, writer, and producer. Kevin Hart was born on July 6, 1979 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, U.S. and is curently age 38. He has started in many movies (listed below) and has received many awards for his outstanding work. His parents are named Henry Witherspoon and Nancy Hart. He is curently not married thought he has had to wives; Eniko Parrish (mid-2016) and Torrei Hart (2003-2011). He has three kids; Hendrix Hart, Heaven Hart, and Kenzo Kash Hart.

Kevin Hart's first stand-up comedy act was at The Laff House in Philadelphia under the name of "Lil Kev". His career suffered a slow start, but started entering comedy competitions in Massachusetts, and his career soon turned for the better. He has dealt with the acting, singing, and comedy industries. He's produced songs such as "Push It On Me" and "Baller Alert" He has also done a little bit of modeling such as the shoot he did with Macy's for underwear. He has appeared in many TV shows such as "The Real Husbands of Hollywood", "The Big House", and "All of Us".
