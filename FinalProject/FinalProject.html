Changing Your Grades

You have the ability to hack into the school computer system. You can change a person's grades. Would you change your own? Why or why not? What if you could change the grade for a basketball player who has a scholarship to play for a big university?

Some students have the ability to hack into the school grading system, allowing themselves to change their grades or their classmates' grades. This situtation that these students have is a true test of their morals.

The real question is: Is Altering You Grades For Your Benefit A Good Thing or A Bad Thing?
